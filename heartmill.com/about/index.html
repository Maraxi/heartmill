Creating cinematic worlds for film, fashion and culture through direction and visual design

Christina Heartmill

is a freelancing art director, photo/videographer and set designer based in Berlin/Germany.

Her work focuses on portrait photography, set design for short/feature films and commercials, as well as creating her own short films and cinematic short content.

She uses dark beauty, surrealism, mystery and phantasy elements to illustrate and explore emotional or cognitive layers of the soul.
