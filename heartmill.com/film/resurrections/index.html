  • A DINNER FOR RESURRECTIONS

    gothic music drama, medium length

    written and directed by : Christina Heartmill, DOP: Lime Reiter

    'A Dinner for Resurrections' follows a young woman seeking love after the death of her mother and the cold distance of her father. Lured into a surreal vampire world, she believes she has found community. But love, power, and desire blur into something darker. A visually rich, experimental tale of longing, illusion, and the hunger to belong.
