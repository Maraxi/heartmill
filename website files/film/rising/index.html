  • THE RISING

    narrative music art film | client work

    Direction, edit & videography: Christina Heartmill

    A romance art film without dialogue, story told through music/lyrics and visuals. A witch falls in love with a soon-to-become-werewolf. After their short lived romance, he faces his destiny: can he be saved?

    This film is based on real life events of the subjects, translated into a romantic fantasy drama and was created to be played at THEIR WEDDING: merging their love for fantasy and a real-life romance into one.
