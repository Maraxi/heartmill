  • Tales of resilience | peaky blinder's sisterhood

    narrative music art film

    direction, set, edit & head of videography: Christina Heartmill

    Set in the 1920s, referencing the criminal gang "Peaky Blinders", three women have to endure abusive situations born from patriarchal issues. Escaping their fate through wit, revenge and reactive violence, they realize that supporting each other is the way to success: a sisterhood is born.

    Personal project. A modern adaption of the 20s and musical tale about toxic masculinity, patriarchy and feminism.
